Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

At Flavorly, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered recipe generation service.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information:

  • Name and email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile information you choose to provide

2. Recipe Preferences:

  • Dietary restrictions and preferences
  • Health status information
  • Cuisine preferences
  • Flavor preferences
  • Available ingredients
  • Dish types you request

3. Usage Data:

  • Recipe generation requests
  • Saved recipes and preferences
  • AI model usage patterns
  • Device and browser information

How We Use Your Information

1. Service Provision:

  • Generate personalized recipes based on your preferences
  • Save and manage your recipe collection
  • Provide customer support

2. Service Improvement:

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve AI recommendations
  • Enhance user experience
  • Develop new features

3. Communication:

  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Share updates about new features

AI Model Data Processing

1. API Communications:

  • Your recipe requests are sent to third-party AI providers (Claude, Grok, Mistral)
  • These providers process your requests to generate recipe suggestions
  • We use your API keys for these communications

2. Data Retention:

  • AI providers may retain request data according to their own policies
  • We recommend reviewing their privacy policies
  • Recipe generation requests are not permanently stored on our servers

3. Provider Policies:

  • Claude (Anthropic): https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
  • Grok (xAI): https://x.ai/privacy
  • Mistral AI: https://www.mistral.ai/privacy

Data Security

1. Encryption:

  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard methods
  • Data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • API keys are stored securely

2. Access Controls:

  • Only authorized personnel can access user data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee training on data protection

3. Data Minimization:

  • We collect only necessary information
  • Automatic deletion of old, unused data
  • Right to request data deletion

Cookies and Tracking

1. Essential Cookies:

  • Maintain user sessions
  • Remember login preferences
  • Ensure site functionality

2. Analytics:

  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Error tracking

3. Control Options:

  • Browser settings to control cookies
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Clear browsing data

Third-Party Services

1. AI Providers:

  • Claude (Anthropic)
  • Grok (xAI)
  • Mistral AI

2. Infrastructure:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Content delivery networks
  • Analytics services

Your Rights

1. Access Rights:

  • View your personal information
  • Download your data
  • Update your profile

2. Deletion Rights:

  • Delete your account
  • Request data removal
  • Clear saved recipes

3. Correction Rights:

  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Update preferences
  • Modify account details

Data Retention

1. Active Accounts:

  • Retain data while account is active
  • Regular security updates
  • Performance optimization

2. Deleted Accounts:

  • Immediate deletion of user data
  • Retention of anonymized usage data
  • Compliance with legal requirements

3. Legal Requirements:

  • Retain data as required by law
  • Cooperate with legal requests
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Children's Privacy

Flavorly is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Changes to This Policy
